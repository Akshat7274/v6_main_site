  • Volunteer

Volunteering in an annual tech fest like siNUsoid V6 is a great way to get connected with people from all around and learn about new technologies while also exploring your management skills. Also, it's a terrific platform to meet new people and create some lifelong experiences. Come, join us and be a part of our volunteer program if you're looking forward to show your support or just want to have some fun and discover your untapped potential. There will be a bunch of events, and we'll work extremely hard to make sure everything goes well. We hope to see you soon!

Team
Descriptions

Content

Good content is not storytelling. It’s telling your story well. But great content goes beyond that - it is a willingness to push boundaries and take risks. The content team of siNUsoid is responsible for ideation, optimization and conversion of ideas into engaging content for our fest and events. Don’t let our vacant stares fool you - us zoning out is us working.

Sponsorship

It’s all about the money, money, money. Our sponsorship team aids in making the events bigger, brighter and better by acquiring new sponsors and maintaining its relationships with old sponsors. We aim to extend the reach of both our university and its sponsors. If you think you can make a valuable addition to the team, please sign up!

Marketing

The best marketing does not feel like marketing. It’s merely the creative use of truths. This is the cradle of countless ideas and a Garden of Eden for all those who’re looking to gain first-hand experience working in marketing."

Operations

The nice thing about teamwork is that you always have someone on your side. The operations team is the backbone of every other team of siNUsoid. They’re the reason every team’s hardwork and passion culminates in a successful event. Are you looking to be an important component in the grand scheme of things? Then, this place calls for you.

Tech

When you hand good people some great possibilities, they do exceptional things. The technical team of siNUsoid embodies this mantra, working our magic behind the screens to build up the technical pillars of the fest. As a tech fest, we emphasise on the aspect of having a strong technical team. If you have the mojo, the coder in you will find home here!

Digital Design

Design is intelligence made visible, and creativity is intelligence having fun. If you want to convert ideas into stunning graphics, the design team of siNUsoid welcomes you with open arms. From Instagram feed to Whatsapp groups and our website to the mails - the visuals are all us. We’re like the Big Brother from George Orwell’s 1984, we’re everywhere!!

Creative

Art is something that makes you breathe with a different kind of happiness. If your hands always reach for the paintbrush, and an empty canvas is just an infinite pool of opportunities, the creative team welcomes you. Responsible for the gorgeous artworks that grace the walls during our three day fest, we are the coterie of innovation.

Event Curation

We do not remember days, but rather moments. The event curation team of siNUsoid strives to organize events that achieves our goals and exceeds every expectation. From planning to execution, we are the brains and the moxie behind the numerous events that siNUsoid V6 organises.

Public Relations

People buy relationships, stories and magic. If that is something you strive to create and promote, the public relations team would love to have you. We’re the first point of contact and we form lasting connections with the people we get into contact with. Sounds jazzy, right? Come join us if you think you’re cut-out for this.
