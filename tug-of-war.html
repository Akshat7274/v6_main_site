  • Tug Of War

Date : 19th November 2022

Venue : Bowl Area

Time : 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM

As the game goes overtime, the tension in the air gets thick. It’s a game we’ve always liked to play since our childhood. In picnics or gatherings it has always been everyone’s favorite and people flocked to find a rope and form teams to battle out. Tug of War is sure to get your adrenaline pumping and we can't wait to see who emerges victorious. It may seem like a game where the strongest wins, but it’s more than a test of strength. Form your team and we’ll see you at the bowl area for this event


If you’re not participating, don’t miss out on cheering for your friends and batchmates!
